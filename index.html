About 1738 Salon

We are Nairobi's leading salon. Located in Yaya Centre, Kilimani area of Nairobi, Kenya we have been offering the best in beauty services and products for more than 20 years

to ladies and gents Our philosophy of “Relax, Refresh, Revive“ ensures that each client who walks through our doors has an enhancing experience to their individual style.

We do this by using the world's best essentials in beauty grooming and personal care. Our highly experienced and trained staff know their trade and take you through a consultation process during each visit to help us understand your individual style. This enables us customize the right service for you. We are committed to offering unrivaled service in hair care and beauty therapy.

History

We opened our doors in 1989 when Yaya Centre Mall first opened its doors to the Public and have remained open ever since. Over the years we have served numerous of clients from different parts of the world, who have not failed to recommend their friends to visit us when in this region.

Initially 1738 had specialised in Caucasian hair and had six hairdressers from six diff countries who worked for more than ten years. Gradually with growing populace on this side of town, it gradually moved to other types of hair: Afro, Asian and others. During this period the six hair dressers managed a skills transfer process to Kenyan hairdressers who now run the show. The salon believes in continuous learning and always organizes training and seminars for its staff to keep them relevant and up to date with market trends.
